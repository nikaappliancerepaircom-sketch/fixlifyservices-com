Frigidaire Refrigerator Repair — Common Issues We Fix

Our technicians have extensive experience with Frigidaire refrigerator repair faults. We diagnose accurately and repair on the first visit in most cases.

Refrigerator Not Cooling

Frigidaire refrigerators are frequent in rental housing and develop cooling failures from dirty condenser coils, failed evaporator fans, or malfunctioning defrost systems. We carry all common Frigidaire cooling components.

Ice Maker Faults

Frigidaire side-by-side refrigerators develop ice maker failures from frozen fill tubes and faulty water inlet valves. The Frigidaire ice maker module is a common replacement part and we stock it on our service vehicles.

Frigidaire Refrigerator Repair FAQ

Is it worth repairing a Frigidaire in a rental property?
Yes, in most cases. Frigidaire parts are inexpensive and widely available. A typical Frigidaire repair costs $100 to $300, well below replacement cost. We can invoice the property management company directly if needed.
Do you repair Frigidaire Gallery appliances?
Yes. The Frigidaire Gallery series is fully serviced including dishwashers, ranges, washers, dryers, and refrigerators. Gallery parts are the same as standard Frigidaire in most cases.
