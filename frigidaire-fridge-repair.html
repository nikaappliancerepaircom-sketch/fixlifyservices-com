Frigidaire Refrigerator Repair in Toronto — In-Depth Guide

Frigidaire refrigerators are among the most common appliances in Toronto's rental housing stock and family homes. The Gallery and Professional series — covering side-by-side, French-door, and top-freezer configurations — share a standardized sealed system and electronic control board that make diagnosis predictable. The single most common Frigidaire refrigerator call in Toronto is not cooling properly, which in over 60% of cases traces to dirty condenser coils. Toronto apartments often have refrigerators pushed against walls with inadequate ventilation clearance, accelerating coil dust accumulation. A thorough coil cleaning restores cooling efficiency and is included with every Fixlify diagnostic visit.

Ice maker failures are the second most frequent Frigidaire refrigerator call across the GTA. Frigidaire side-by-side models from 2012–2020 have a documented issue with the water fill tube freezing above the ice maker — a design characteristic amplified by Toronto's cold winters when garage-stored fridges operate in ambient temperatures below 10°C. Our technicians clear frozen fill tubes with a heat gun, insulate the tube, and adjust the fill timing to prevent recurrence. If the ice maker module itself has failed (common after 6–8 years), we carry Frigidaire OEM ice maker assemblies for the most popular side-by-side models on the van for same-day replacement.

The dH (defrost heater) and dF (defrost thermostat) codes on Frigidaire electronic control models indicate the defrost system has failed, allowing ice to accumulate on the evaporator coil until airflow stops completely. This produces a symptom pattern of the freezer staying cold but the fresh-food section warming — a classic evaporator ice-over presentation. Fixlify technicians manually defrost the evaporator, test the heater resistance, and confirm the thermostat continuity before replacing the failed component. A complete defrost system repair typically takes 90 minutes and costs $120–$240 including parts and labour.
