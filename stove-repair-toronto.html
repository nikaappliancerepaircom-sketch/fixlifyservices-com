Stove Maintenance Tips to Prevent Costly Repairs

Regular maintenance extends your stove's lifespan and prevents the most common breakdowns we see in Toronto homes. Follow these five tips from our experienced technicians:

  1. Clean burner grates and drip pans after every heavy cooking session ‚Äî grease buildup is the leading cause of stovetop fires in Ontario homes
  2. For gas stoves, inspect burner ports monthly and clear any clogs with a pin ‚Äî blocked ports cause uneven flames and wasted gas
  3. Check that electric coil elements sit flat in their receptacles ‚Äî a tilted element causes uneven heating and accelerates receptacle burnout
  4. For glass cooktops, use flat-bottom cookware only and lift pots rather than sliding ‚Äî dragging scratches the surface and can lead to cracks
  5. Have gas connections professionally inspected every 2 years ‚Äî flex connectors and shut-off valves age and should be replaced every 10 years per TSSA guidelines
