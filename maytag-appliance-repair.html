Maytag Appliance Repair — Built Tough, Fixed Right

Maytag has earned its reputation as one of North America's most dependable appliance brands. Founded in 1893 and now part of the Whirlpool Corporation family, Maytag appliances are engineered with commercial-grade components that deliver years of heavy-duty performance. When a Maytag appliance does need service, it takes a technician who understands their robust internal engineering — and Fixlify delivers exactly that.

Our certified technicians service Maytag washers, dryers, refrigerators, dishwashers, and ranges throughout Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area. We carry genuine Maytag and Whirlpool-compatible parts in our service vehicles, enabling same-day repairs for the majority of common issues.

What Makes Maytag Different from Other Brands

Maytag appliances are built with their proprietary Commercial Technology drive system in washers, a PowerDry cycle in dryers that uses extra heating elements for faster drying, and a PowerCold feature in refrigerators that rapidly chills the interior after the door has been opened. These specialized systems require technicians who understand Maytag's unique engineering. Our team is trained on the full Maytag product line, from their top-load and front-load washers to their side-by-side and French-door refrigerators.

Because Maytag is manufactured by Whirlpool Corporation, many internal components are shared across both brands. This means parts availability is excellent, and our technicians' deep Whirlpool platform expertise transfers directly to every Maytag repair.

Common Maytag Repairs We Handle

  • Maytag washer not spinning or agitating — drive motor and clutch assembly repair
  • Maytag dryer not heating — thermal fuse, heating element, and gas valve replacement
  • Maytag refrigerator not cooling — compressor relay, evaporator fan, and defrost system repair
  • Maytag dishwasher not draining — pump motor, check valve, and drain hose service
  • Maytag oven not heating evenly — bake element, temperature sensor, and control board repair
  • Maytag washer error codes — F5E2 lid lock, F7E1 motor, F8E1 water supply diagnostics

Popular Maytag Models We Service in Toronto

Our technicians regularly repair the Maytag MHW8630H front-load washer, Maytag MEDB955F dryer, Maytag MFI2570FE French-door refrigerator, Maytag MDB8959SK dishwasher, and Maytag MER8800F electric range. We also service the Maytag Bravos and Centennial series top-load washers, which remain extremely popular in Toronto-area homes. Whether your Maytag is a 2024 model or a decade-old workhorse, we have the parts and expertise to get it running again.
