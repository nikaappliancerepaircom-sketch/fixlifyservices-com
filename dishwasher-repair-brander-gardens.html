Hard water scaling / poor cleaning
EPCOR's 170–200 mg/L hard water calcifies spray arm nozzles and heating elements in Brander Gardens homes over 8–12 years. We descale and restore spray pressure, or replace blocked spray arms.
Not draining / standing water
Drain pump wear or clogged filter housing. Extremely common in Whirlpool and Kenmore units from the 1980s–90s build era. We carry pump assemblies as standard truck stock for Brander Gardens calls.
Door latch failure
Plastic latch housing cracks after repeated thermal cycling in older units. Dishwasher refuses to start when the door switch fails to signal. Quick OEM latch swap, very common in 25–35 year-old Brander Gardens units.
Heating element failure
Dishes exit wet or cold — heating element has burned out. Mineral deposits from EPCOR hard water can accelerate element degradation in Brander Gardens homes without regular descaling.
Control board / E-code faults
Newer Bosch and Samsung units installed in Brander Gardens kitchen renovations generate error codes (E15, 4C, OE). We diagnose and resolve water inlet, drain, and motor faults same-day.
Wash motor failure
Dishwasher runs but dishes come out dirty — wash motor has seized or worn out. We assess motor health during diagnostic and quote repair vs. replacement based on the unit's overall condition.
