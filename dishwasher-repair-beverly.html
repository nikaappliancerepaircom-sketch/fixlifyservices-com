Common Dishwasher Problems We Fix in Beverly

Beverly’s older appliance stock creates a specific repair profile — wear-related failures rather than electronics, vintage part sourcing, and hard-water accumulation on units that have rarely been serviced.

Vintage Kenmore pump failure
Pre-2017 Sears-era Kenmore drain pump wear. We source discontinued Kenmore-compatible pump kits. Common across Beverly’s bungalow stock where original 1990s–2000s units are still in service.
Severe hard-water scaling
EPCOR’s 170–200 mg/L water on 15+ year old units without regular maintenance creates severe mineral deposits. We descale, restore spray pressure, and assess total unit condition.
Not draining / standing water
Drain pump failure or blocked drain line. Older Frigidaire FDB series and Whirlpool built-under models are prone to pump wear after 12–15 years. Same-day fix in most cases.
Door gasket / seal failure
Rubber door seals degrade over time, especially on older units. We source replacement gaskets for older Kenmore and Frigidaire models and complete the replacement in under an hour.
Heating element burnout
Dishes wet or not sanitizing. Heating element burnout is common on 12–20 year old units. We carry elements for older Whirlpool and Frigidaire platforms as standard Beverly truck stock.
Control board / timer failure
Older mechanical timer controls and early electronic boards fail with age. We diagnose and source replacements for vintage control systems where cost-effective.
