Why We Cover the Entire GTA

The Greater Toronto Area spans four regional municipalities and hundreds of distinct communities — from the downtown Toronto condo towers that line the waterfront to the sprawling suburban subdivisions of Brampton and the lakefront estates of Oakville. Appliances break down everywhere, and a flat tire on the 401 or traffic on the 427 should not be the reason your refrigerator sits unrepaired for three days. That is why Fixlify Appliance Services maintains technicians dispatched from multiple hubs across the GTA.

In the City of Toronto, we cover all former boroughs: the downtown core, Scarborough, North York, Etobicoke, East York, and York. Our technicians understand the unique challenges of each zone — compact stackable units in King West condos, post-war built-in ranges in the Annex, and high-capacity washers in large Etobicoke detached homes all need different approaches. We stock the parts and carry the knowledge for all of them.

In Peel Region, our Mississauga and Brampton coverage is extensive. The Square One condo district in Mississauga requires a different parts inventory than the large-family homes of Springdale in Brampton. Our trucks serving these areas carry a broader range of capacities — from slim Bosch dishwasher circulation pumps to high-capacity Whirlpool washer bearings. York Region cities — Vaughan, Markham, and Richmond Hill — see a high proportion of newer homes with Samsung and LG appliance suites, which our York Region technicians are specialized to service.

Halton Region, including Oakville and Burlington, features a higher concentration of premium built-in appliances from KitchenAid, Miele, and Wolf. These upscale brands require factory-spec diagnostic tools and OEM parts — both of which we maintain. Durham Region, from Pickering through Oshawa, is served with slightly longer arrival windows but still same-day capability for most bookings made before noon. Across all GTA locations, repair costs range from $80 to $400 depending on the appliance and fault type, with upfront flat-rate pricing before any work begins.
