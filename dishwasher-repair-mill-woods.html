Common issues

Common Dishwasher Problems We Fix in Mill Woods

These are the dishwasher faults we diagnose most often in Mill Woods — weighted toward older Kenmore, Maytag, and Whirlpool units common in 1970s–80s builds, plus the growing share of Samsung and LG installed in recent kitchen renovations.

Not draining / standing water
Drain pump failure or worn impeller — extremely common in Kenmore and Maytag units over 12 years old. We carry the pump assembly on the truck for same-day fix in Burnewood and Richfield homes.
Door latch not engaging
Plastic latch housing cracks on older units. Dishwasher won't start if the latch doesn't signal correctly. 30-minute OEM latch replacement, very common in Mill Woods Town Centre and Lakewood.
Spray arm scaling / not cleaning
Edmonton hard water calcifies nozzles after 8–12 years. Older Kenmore and Whirlpool units in Ridgewood and Burnewood show heavy calcium deposits that block water flow and leave dishes dirty.
Heating element failure
Dishes come out wet, water lukewarm. Heating element ohms out at infinity — replacement required. Common in Mill Woods units aged 10–18 years, particularly Maytag and early Whirlpool models.
Samsung / LG error codes
Newer Samsung and LG dishwashers installed during Mill Woods kitchen renovations throw 4C, OE, and LE error codes. We diagnose water supply faults, drain failures, and motor issues same-day.
Detergent dispenser stuck
Spring-loaded dispenser door fails to open during wash cycle — dishes come out with undissolved detergent residue. Replacement dispenser assembly is a standard repair in older Mill Woods units.
