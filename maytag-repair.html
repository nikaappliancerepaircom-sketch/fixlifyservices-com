Common Maytag Appliance Problems We Fix

Our technicians have repaired thousands of Maytag appliances across Toronto and the GTA. These are the most frequent faults we encounter:

Washer Not Draining

Maytag top-load washers develop drain pump clogging from lint and foreign objects, and front-load models show F21 error codes indicating slow drain. We clear pump filters and replace drain pumps as needed.

Dryer Not Heating

Maytag dryers develop heating failures from blown thermal fuses, failed heating elements (electric), or faulty igniters (gas). We carry Maytag-specific heating components for same-day repair.

Refrigerator Defrost Problems

Maytag side-by-side and French Door refrigerators develop frost buildup on evaporator coils from failed defrost heaters, defrost thermostats, or control board timer issues. We test and replace the full defrost circuit.

Dishwasher Not Cleaning

Maytag dishwashers lose cleaning effectiveness from worn spray arm bearings, failing wash pumps, or a faulty water inlet valve. We inspect the full wash system and replace worn components.

Washer Agitator Not Working

Maytag top-load agitator washers develop agitator directional cog failures — a common and inexpensive repair. If the drum spins but the agitator does not move, the cogs need replacement.

Oven Control Board Failure

Maytag electric ranges develop control board failures that cause incorrect temperature, display errors, or burners not responding. We test boards before replacing them to confirm the diagnosis.

Maytag Repair FAQ

Do you repair older Maytag washers with agitators?
Yes. Maytag top-load agitator washers from the 1990s to 2000s are very repairable. The agitator cog kit is one of our most common Maytag repairs and costs around $80 to $120 including labour. Call (437) 524-1053 for same-day service.
My Maytag dryer shows F01 error. What is wrong?
F01 on Maytag dryers indicates a main control board fault. Before replacing the board we check the thermal fuse and wiring harness, as these can cause a false F01 reading. We diagnose before we replace.
Is Maytag a reliable brand worth repairing?
Maytag has one of the best long-term reliability records in the industry. Most Maytag machines last 15 to 18 years with occasional repairs. Repairing a Maytag is almost always the right choice given the parts availability and build quality.
