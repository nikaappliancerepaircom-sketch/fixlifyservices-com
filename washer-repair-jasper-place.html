Common issues

Common Washer Problems We Fix in Jasper Place

Won't spin / weak spin
Lid switch failure on top-loaders, worn clutch, or motor coupling breaks are the leading causes in older Whirlpool and Kenmore direct-drive machines common in Jasper Place. Usually a same-day fix.
Not draining / water standing
Drain pump blockage or pump failure. Front-loaders collect lint and debris in the pump filter — we clear and test the drain system. Edmonton's harder water accelerates pump wear on older top-loaders.
Noisy / banging during spin
Drum bearing failure in front-loaders or worn tub bearing in top-loaders produces grinding or rumbling. Common in front-load Samsung and LG units after 8–12 years. We replace bearing assemblies same-day where parts are stocked.
Front-load door seal leak
Door boot gasket tears or develops mold in Jasper Place's older front-load units. Water leaks onto laundry room floor. OEM gasket replacement is a standard repair on Whirlpool, Samsung, and LG front-loaders.
Washer won't start
Control board failure, door lock assembly, or timer faults prevent the cycle from initiating. We diagnose with manufacturer tools and replace only the failed component rather than the full control assembly where possible.
F / E error codes
Modern Samsung, LG, and Whirlpool washers display error codes for water supply, drain, spin, and motor faults. We carry the diagnostic tools to read codes, identify root cause, and repair on the first visit where parts permit.
