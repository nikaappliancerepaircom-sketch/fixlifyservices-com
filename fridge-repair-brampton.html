Maintenance Tips for Refrigerator Owners in Brampton

Preventive maintenance is the most cost-effective way to extend your refrigerator's lifespan and avoid emergency repair calls. These five tips are specifically relevant for Brampton homes, where secondary basement kitchens are common plays a significant role in appliance wear:

  1. Vacuum the condenser coils on the back or bottom of the fridge every 6 months ‚Äî dust-clogged coils make the compressor work 30% harder
  2. Check the door gasket seal by closing the door on a dollar bill ‚Äî if the bill slides out easily, the gasket needs replacement
  3. Keep the fridge between 1-4 degrees C and the freezer at -18 degrees C ‚Äî use a separate thermometer to verify the built-in display
  4. Do not overstuff the fridge ‚Äî airflow between items is essential for even cooling; leave at least 1 inch of clearance on each shelf
  5. Clean the defrost drain hole (usually at the back of the fridge compartment) every 3 months to prevent clogs and water leaks

Following these steps can extend your refrigerator's life by 3-5 years and reduce the likelihood of an unexpected breakdown.
