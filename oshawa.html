Oshawa Appliance Repair Considerations

Oshawa receives its municipal water from Durham Region's Lake Ontario treatment system. While the water is safe and well-treated, it carries enough mineral content to gradually build scale deposits inside dishwashers, washing machines, and refrigerator water lines ‚Äî particularly in homes with older internal plumbing that may add trace sediment. We recommend annual descaling for all water-connected appliances. The temperature extremes between harsh Durham Region winters and humid summers accelerate wear on rubber gaskets, door seals, and dryer vent connections. Homes in south Oshawa with original plumbing should also have washing machine supply hoses inspected annually, as aging brass fittings and rubber lines are the most common source of preventable flooding.
