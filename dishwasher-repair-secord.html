LG dishwashers in Secord generate AE (water leakage) and OE (drain) fault codes at the highest frequency we see in this age bracket. AE codes activate when the machine's flood sensor detects moisture in the base pan — often from a worn door seal allowing drops of water to escape during cycles rather than a catastrophic flood. We inspect the door gasket, sump housing, and pump connections before deciding on repair approach: most AE resolutions involve a door seal replacement and base pan dry-out. OE drain codes point to a clogged drain pump filter or failing pump motor — typically a $140 to $200 fix completed in a single visit.

Edmonton's water hardness at 130–160 mg/L hits Secord's builder-grade dishwashers particularly hard because these units lack the water-softening features found in premium Bosch or Miele dishwashers. Without a dedicated water softener or regular descaling tablet use, spray nozzles in Samsung and LG dishwashers become partially blocked within 12 to 18 months, reducing wash pressure and leaving food residue and white scale deposits on dishes. Our technicians descale spray arms, replace check valves where worn, and clean sump filters in every service call — resulting in a genuinely restored machine rather than a temporary fix. We also recommend a preventive filter clean every 90 days to homeowners in Secord.

Dishwasher Repair or Replace? A Guide for Secord Homeowners

When your dishwasher breaks down in Secord, the repair-vs-replace decision comes down to three factors: the age of the unit, the cost of the repair, and the current replacement cost. Here is the framework our technicians use:

The 50% Rule

If the repair cost exceeds 50% of the replacement price of a comparable new unit, replacing usually makes more financial sense. A basic entry-level dishwasher in Edmonton runs $550–$900 installed. If your repair quote is under $350, repairing is almost always the right call.

Repair is the better choice when the unit is under 10 years old, the repair involves a single component (drain pump, door latch, heating element), and the brand is Bosch, KitchenAid, Miele, or Electrolux — brands with higher replacement costs and longer lifespans.

Replacement may make more sense when the dishwasher is 12–15+ years old, you face multiple simultaneous failures, or the control board on a budget brand costs $300+ to replace.

Our Secord technicians give you an honest assessment during the diagnostic visit. We will tell you when replacement is the smarter financial decision rather than recommending a costly repair on a unit near end of life.

Dishwasher Maintenance Tips for Secord Homes

Edmonton's hard water is particularly hard on dishwashers. At 170–200 mg/L, the mineral content in EPCOR-supplied water is significantly above the national average of 120 mg/L. Secord homeowners who follow a simple maintenance routine extend their dishwasher's life by 3–5 years. Here is what we recommend based on the repairs we see most often:

  • Monthly: Run an empty hot cycle with white vinegar or a citric-acid tablet (Finish or Cascade dishwasher cleaner). This dissolves mineral deposits in spray arms, the heating element, and the detergent dispenser — the most critical maintenance step with Edmonton water.
  • Every 2 months: Remove and rinse the filter basket. A clogged filter is the leading cause of draining failures and poor wash performance in Secord dishwashers. Takes 2 minutes.
  • Quarterly: Check the door gasket for cracks or brittleness. Secord winters mean the door seal experiences temperature cycling — a cracked gasket leads to leaks that damage kitchen floors.
  • Always: Use rinse aid. In Edmonton's hard water, rinse aid is not optional — it prevents scale spotting on glassware and helps the drying cycle perform correctly. Refill every 3–4 weeks.

If you notice any change in cleaning quality, draining, or unusual sounds, call us early. A $65 diagnostic caught before failure is far less expensive than a $280 pump replacement after it seizes.
