What We Fix

Common Fridge Faults in Beverly

Frost Buildup in Freezer

Older top-freezer fridges in Beverly develop heavy frost buildup when the defrost heater, defrost thermostat, or defrost timer fails. The freezer frosts over, the fridge warms up, or the fan can't push air through the frost. All three defrost components are inexpensive and straightforward to replace.

Fridge Not Cooling (Warm Section)

If the freezer stays cold but the fridge section warms up, the evaporator fan motor has usually failed — or the defrost system has iced over the evaporator coil, blocking airflow. Both are common in 15–25 year old GE and Kenmore units and both are cost-effective repairs.

Door Gasket Failure

Rubber door gaskets on older fridges harden and crack over time, letting warm air in and causing the compressor to run continuously. A door gasket replacement ($80–$130) is one of the best-value repairs on an older fridge — it also significantly reduces electricity use.

Thermostat / Temperature Control

Mechanical thermostats in older Kenmore and GE fridges fail in two ways: the fridge stops cooling entirely, or it freezes food in the fridge section. Thermostat replacement is a $95–$145 repair and restores precise temperature control.

Ice Maker (Older Kenmore)

Older Kenmore ice maker modules fail to cycle — they stop producing ice but the water supply is fine. We assess whether the ice maker module itself is worth replacing given the fridge's age, or whether disabling it and using a tray is the more practical solution.

Water Pooling Under Fridge

Water on the floor under a fridge is usually a blocked defrost drain — not a refrigerant leak. We clear the drain tube, check the drain pan, and test that defrost water is routing correctly. A quick fix that prevents floor and cabinet water damage.

Fridge Repair Questions — Beverly

Is my 20-year-old Kenmore fridge worth repairing? +
Often yes. Older Kenmore top-freezer fridges are mechanically simple and repair costs are low. Defrost heater, door gasket, fan motor, and thermostat repairs typically run $80–$200 — almost always worth it on a well-maintained machine. If the compressor has failed, that's a different calculation and we'll advise honestly on whether replacement makes more sense.
Why is there frost built up inside my freezer? +
Excessive frost in the freezer compartment means the automatic defrost system has failed — either the defrost heater, defrost thermostat, or the defrost timer/control. The frost prevents airflow and eventually causes the fridge section to warm up. All three components are inexpensive and the repair is straightforward.

Why is water pooling under my fridge? +

Common Fridge Problems We Fix in Beverly

Refrigerator repair calls in Beverly peak in two seasons: summer, when compressors work harder against the heat, and late fall, when Edmonton's temperature drops rapidly and garage fridges begin cycling abnormally. These are the faults our technicians encounter most often in Beverly:

  • →Fridge not cooling or not cold enough — compressor, thermostat, evaporator fan, or condenser coil failure. The evaporator fan is the most common single-point failure on modern frost-free units in Beverly.
  • →Ice building up on the back wall — a failed defrost heater or defrost thermostat causes ice to accumulate on the evaporator. This blocks airflow and the fridge gradually warms up. Very common in Beverly during winter months.
  • →Water dispenser or ice maker not working — a frozen fill line or failed water inlet valve. Edmonton homes with in-door dispensers see frozen lines regularly during the coldest weeks of winter.
  • →Fridge running constantly or spiking energy bill — a worn door gasket lets cold air escape and forces the compressor to run continuously. Test: close the door on a sheet of paper — if it slides out easily, the gasket is failing.
  • →Clicking or not starting — a failing start relay is an inexpensive fix ($60–$110) that prevents a full compressor replacement ($450–$750). Catching it early saves significant money.

A non-functioning fridge is a food safety emergency. We prioritize same-day service for fridge calls in Beverly — book before noon and we aim to arrive that afternoon.
