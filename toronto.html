Common Appliance Issues We Fix in Toronto

Lake Ontario's hard water supply accelerates limescale buildup inside dishwasher spray arms, washing machine inlet valves, and refrigerator water dispensers. We see these mineral-related failures significantly more often in Toronto than in cities with softer water. Seasonal temperature swings also stress appliances: basement laundry rooms in older homes can drop below 15 degrees Celsius in January, causing washing machine drain pumps to strain and refrigerator compressors to cycle inefficiently.
