Our Mission

Appliance Repair, Redesigned from the Ground Up

We launched Fixlify in 2017 with one goal: bring modern technology and honest service to an industry stuck in the past. Here is how we do it differently.

💡

Diagnostic Technology That Gets It Right the First Time

Traditional appliance repair often means a technician showing up, eyeballing the problem, and hoping for the best. Fixlify takes a different approach. Our technicians carry modern diagnostic equipment that reads error codes directly from your appliance's control board, measures electrical resistance across components, and pinpoints failures before a single part is ordered. This data-driven process means we identify the root cause on the first visit — not the second or third. Whether it is a Samsung refrigerator compressor drawing too many amps or a Bosch dishwasher with a faulty drain pump sensor, our tools tell us exactly what is wrong. That precision translates into faster repairs, fewer return visits, and lower costs for you.

💰

Transparent Pricing Before Any Wrench Turns

Nobody likes being surprised by a repair bill. Every Fixlify service call begins with a detailed digital quote sent straight to your phone. You see the exact cost of parts, the labour charge, and the 90-day warranty coverage — all before we start any work. Most residential appliance repairs in Toronto fall between $80 and $400, and we give you the specific number upfront. If the quote does not work for you, there is no pressure and no charge beyond the diagnostic fee. We have built our reputation on this transparency since 2017, and it is why our customers keep calling us back.

⚡

Real-Time Scheduling and Same-Day Dispatch

When your refrigerator stops cooling at 7 AM and you have $300 worth of groceries at risk, you cannot wait three days for an appointment. Fixlify's online booking system shows live technician availability across the entire GTA in real time. Pick a slot that works for your schedule, get an instant text confirmation, and receive a 30-minute heads-up call before your technician arrives. Bookings placed before 2 PM typically receive same-day service within 2 to 4 hours. We dispatch from multiple staging points across Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Scarborough, and North York so our response time stays fast no matter where you live.

🤝

A Digital Workflow from Booking to Invoice

Everything at Fixlify runs through our digital platform. You book online, receive automated appointment reminders by text, get a digital quote on your phone, approve it with one tap, and receive a detailed digital invoice after the repair is complete. No paper, no phone tag, no lost receipts. Your full service history is stored in our system, so if you ever need warranty service or a follow-up, we already have every detail on file. This is what modern appliance repair looks like — convenient, transparent, and built around your time.

Credentials

Fully Licensed, Insured & Verified

Credentials matter when someone is working inside your home. Here is what every Fixlify technician brings to the job.

🏅

Ontario-Licensed Technicians

Every technician holds a valid appliance service licence under Ontario technical standards. We maintain current credentials and pursue ongoing training on new appliance platforms.

🔥

TSSA Gas Certification

Our gas appliance specialists carry G2 and G3 certifications from the Technical Standards and Safety Authority, required by Ontario law for gas range, oven, dryer, and cooktop repairs.

🛡

Comprehensive Liability Insurance

We carry full general liability coverage on every service call. If anything goes wrong during a repair, you are protected. This is non-negotiable for any technician on the Fixlify roster.

Criminal Background Verified

All team members pass a thorough criminal background check before being assigned to any customer call. We understand the trust involved in letting someone into your home and we take that responsibility seriously.
