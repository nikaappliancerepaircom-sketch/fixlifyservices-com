Common Frigidaire Appliance Problems We Fix

Our technicians have repaired thousands of Frigidaire appliances across Toronto and the GTA. These are the most frequent faults we encounter:

Refrigerator Not Cooling

Frigidaire refrigerators are frequent in rental housing and develop cooling failures from dirty condenser coils, failed evaporator fans, or malfunctioning defrost systems. We carry all common Frigidaire cooling components.

Ice Maker Faults

Frigidaire side-by-side refrigerators develop ice maker failures from frozen fill tubes and faulty water inlet valves. The Frigidaire ice maker module is a common replacement part and we stock it on our service vehicles.

Washer Not Starting

Frigidaire washers develop start failures from failed lid switches (top-load) or door latches (front-load), faulty control boards, or timer malfunctions in older models. We diagnose the full start circuit.

Oven Not Heating

Frigidaire electric ranges with failed bake elements are common calls. The Frigidaire bake element has a high failure rate after 5 to 8 years of use. We carry elements for Gallery and Professional series models.

Dishwasher Drainage Issues

Frigidaire dishwashers develop drainage problems from clogged pump filters and failed drain pumps. The i30 error code indicates a drainage fault. We clear blockages and replace pumps as needed.

Dryer Not Heating

Frigidaire dryers develop heating failures from blown thermal fuses, failed heating elements, or a faulty cycling thermostat. These are all inexpensive parts that restore heating quickly.
