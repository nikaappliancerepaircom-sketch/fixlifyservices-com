How We Define "Reliability" Here

A reliable appliance, from a repair company's perspective, is one that: (1) doesn't require service calls in its first 5 years, (2) doesn't develop the same recurring problem repeatedly, and (3) has parts that are available within a reasonable timeframe when repair is eventually needed. All three factors matter to a homeowner's actual experience.

Ovens and Stoves: What We See

Electric ranges from Frigidaire, GE, and Whirlpool tend to have the lowest service call rates. Gas ranges add igniter-related calls, but otherwise similar patterns. High-end induction ranges from Bosch and Miele are reliable but expensive to repair when control boards fail.
