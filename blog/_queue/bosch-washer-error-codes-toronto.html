Complete Bosch Washer Error Code Reference

Bosch front-load washers use a standardized error code system across their Series 4, Series 6, and Series 8 lines. The codes displayed are "E" followed by a two-digit number. Here are the codes our technicians encounter most frequently in Toronto service calls:

Code Fault Most Common Cause
E17 Water fill timeout Kinked supply hose, clogged inlet filter, failed water inlet valve
E18 Drain fault Clogged pump filter, blocked drain hose, failed drain pump

Diagnosing Bosch E17 — Water Fill Fault

E17 appears when the machine does not reach the target water level within 3–8 minutes (timeout varies by model). The diagnostic sequence our technicians follow:

1

Supply Pressure and Hose Check

Supply valve confirmed fully open. Hose inspected for kinking behind machine. Supply pressure measured — Bosch requires minimum 15 PSI, recommended 30–120 PSI. Low municipal pressure during peak hours can trigger intermittent E17 without component failure.

2

Inlet Valve Mesh Filter Inspection

Fine mesh filters inside the inlet valve connection point are removed and inspected. Toronto's hard water deposits sediment on these filters over time. Clogged filters are cleaned or replaced — often resolving E17 without valve replacement.

3

Water Inlet Valve Solenoid Resistance Test

Multimeter measures solenoid resistance. Bosch inlet valve solenoids typically read 3–5 kilohms. An open circuit or significantly out-of-range reading confirms valve replacement required ($150–$220 CAD).

Diagnosing Bosch E18 — Drain Fault

E18 is the most common Bosch error code we encounter in Toronto. In approximately 60% of E18 cases, cleaning the pump filter (located behind the lower access panel at the front of the machine) resolves the fault with no parts required. The filter collects coins, hair, and debris over months of use and restricts pump flow until the machine cannot empty within the programmed window.

If filter cleaning does not resolve E18, the technician tests the drain pump motor resistance (typically 150–300 ohms for Bosch pumps) and checks for a partial blockage in the drain hose or impeller. A seized pump impeller — sometimes caused by a foreign object wedged in the impeller chamber — reads as normal resistance but produces no flow under power.

Diagnosing Bosch E27 — Door Lock Fault

E27 indicates the door lock circuit is not completing. The Bosch door lock assembly integrates a bimetal thermal actuator and two or three microswitch contacts. The technician tests each switch contact for continuity in both door open and door closed positions. Common failure mode: the wire harness breaks at the door hinge flex point after years of door-opening cycles. This is a wiring repair rather than a component replacement, typically costing $95–$140 CAD.

Do not force open a Bosch washer door when E27 is displayed. Attempting to manually override the door lock on a Bosch front-loader can damage the door hook, strike plate, and in some cases the outer tub hinge. The E27 code may appear because the drum still contains water — opening the door forcefully risks a significant water release. Call a technician to safely drain the machine and diagnose the lock fault.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Bosch washer error code E18 mean?
E18 is a drain fault — the machine did not empty within the expected timeframe. Causes include a clogged pump filter (the most common and easiest fix), a blocked drain hose, or a failed drain pump. Our technicians check the filter first (accessible without tools on most Bosch models), then test pump motor resistance to identify whether the pump itself has failed.
What does Bosch washer error code E17 mean?
E17 indicates the machine did not fill with water within the programmed timeout. Causes include a kinked or frozen water supply hose, a clogged inlet valve mesh filter, low household water pressure, or a failed water inlet valve solenoid. The inlet valve solenoid is tested with a multimeter — correct resistance is typically 3–5 kilohms for Bosch inlet valve solenoids.
How do I reset a Bosch washing machine error code?
After fixing the underlying fault, most Bosch error codes clear by turning the program dial to the Off position, waiting 30 seconds, and restarting. If the code persists after an apparent fix, a stored fault in the control module may need clearing with a service mode reset — our technicians use the Bosch BSH diagnostic procedure to confirm all sensors are reading correctly before signing off.
