Most Common Appliance Repairs in Sherwood Park

Based on our service history in Sherwood Park and the broader Strathcona County area, these are the appliance faults we see most frequently in Sherwood Park homes:

Refrigerator Not Cooling — Sherwood Park

Sherwood Park homes tend to have newer refrigerators than mature Edmonton neighbourhoods, given that much of the community's residential development occurred after 2000. French-door LG and Samsung refrigerators, common in Emerald Hills and Summerwood, are the most frequent refrigerator repair requests. The most common faults are evaporator fan failures and Samsung-specific ice maker assembly ice-over issues. Strathcona County's water hardness (slightly higher than Edmonton core at 180–210 mg/L) makes ice maker scale deposits more common than average. We carry LG and Samsung OEM parts for same-day repairs across most Sherwood Park postal codes (T8A, T8B, T8H).

Washer Not Draining or Spinning — Sherwood Park

Front-load washers in Sherwood Park's newer homes run on high-efficiency cycles that stress door latch assemblies and drain pump seals over time. LG and Samsung front-loaders are the most common platforms. We see frequent drain pump failures and door latch issues in Bosch machines as well, particularly in Foxboro and Woodbridge Farm where Bosch appliances are popular in higher-end kitchen and laundry renovations. Same-day drain pump and door latch repairs are available with OEM parts on our service vehicles.

Dryer Not Heating — Sherwood Park

Natural gas dryers are nearly universal in Sherwood Park's detached homes, connected to ATCO Gas. The most frequent gas dryer repair is gas valve coil kit failure on Whirlpool-platform machines (Whirlpool, Maytag, Kenmore) — the dominant dryer platform in Sherwood Park homes built between 1995 and 2015. The distinctive symptom is a dryer that ignites briefly then fails to sustain heat. Gas valve coil kit replacement is a 45-minute repair we complete same-day in Sherwood Park.

Dishwasher Not Cleaning or Draining — Sherwood Park

Strathcona County's moderately hard water affects dishwashers in Sherwood Park more than some Edmonton neighbourhoods. Spray arm clogging (mineral deposits blocking nozzle holes) is a frequent complaint, as is drain pump buildup. Bosch dishwashers, common in Sherwood Park renovations, have specific error codes (E24 drain, E09 heater) that our technicians diagnose efficiently. Miele dishwashers — appearing more frequently in newer Sherwood Park luxury builds — require brand-specific diagnostics we support fully.

Gas Range Burner Faults — Sherwood Park

Gas ranges in Sherwood Park homes connect to ATCO Gas. The most common faults are igniter failures and ignition switch module issues, often caused by boil-overs on open-burner designs. Samsung, GE, Bosch, and Frigidaire gas ranges are prevalent. Surface burner igniter replacements are same-day repairs in Sherwood Park. Gas oven igniter replacement and oven thermistor service are also completed in a single visit.
