Why Book Appliance Repair Online with Fixlify?

Booking appliance repair online with Fixlify takes less than two minutes. You select your appliance type, enter your Toronto or GTA address, choose a 2-hour arrival window that fits your schedule, and receive instant text and email confirmation. There are no phone queues, no waiting for callbacks, and no uncertainty about whether your booking was received. The entire process is handled digitally through the Fixlify platform.

Same-day slots are available seven days a week when you book before 2 PM. Our technicians are dispatched from locations throughout Toronto, Scarborough, North York, Etobicoke, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Richmond Hill, and Oakville, so arrival times are typically 1–3 hours. If you book the night before, morning slots often open up the following day. The calendar above updates in real time to reflect actual technician availability in your area.

Typical appliance repair costs range from $80 to $400 depending on the appliance type and the nature of the fault. Refrigerator compressor relay replacements, washer drain pump repairs, dryer heating element swaps, dishwasher pump seal jobs, and oven igniter replacements are among the most common calls we handle. For most of these, our technicians carry the required parts on the truck, allowing same-visit completion. Pricing is provided upfront before any work begins — you only pay if you approve the repair.

Every repair completed by Fixlify includes a 90-day warranty on both parts and labour. If the same issue recurs within the warranty period, we return at no additional cost. Our technicians are fully licensed under Ontario's technical standards, carry current liability insurance, and pass thorough background screening. The same care that goes into your repair also goes into the billing — you receive a digital invoice immediately after the job, with a clear breakdown of parts and labour.

Fixlify serves all major brands: Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, GE, Bosch, Frigidaire, Kenmore, Miele, Maytag, and KitchenAid. Whether you have a French-door Samsung refrigerator in a Yonge Street condo, a Whirlpool top-load washer in a Brampton detached home, or a Bosch dishwasher in a Mississauga townhouse, our technicians are trained and equipped for your specific appliance.
