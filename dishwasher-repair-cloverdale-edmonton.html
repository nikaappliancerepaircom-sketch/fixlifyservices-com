Common Dishwasher Problems We Fix in Cloverdale

Hard water scale / spray arm scaling
EPCOR’s 170–200 mg/L water affects all Cloverdale dishwashers — vintage Kenmore and premium Bosch alike. We descale and restore spray pressure same-day.
Bosch E-code / drainage fault
Bosch E24 and E25 drain faults common in Cloverdale’s renovated kitchens. We diagnose Bosch-specific drain pump and filter assembly issues accurately.
Not cleaning / poor wash results
Spray arm blockage, detergent dispenser failure, or water inlet valve restriction. Affects both older Kenmore and newer Miele. Same-day diagnosis and repair.
Not draining / standing water
Drain pump failure on Kenmore and GE units (15–20 years old) or blocked filter on Bosch. We carry drain pump kits for common Cloverdale platforms.
Miele / Bosch door latch or control fault
European brand control and latch issues require brand-specific diagnostic knowledge. We diagnose Miele and Bosch control boards and door latch assemblies accurately.
Leaking from door or base
Door gasket degradation on older Kenmore, or pump seal on Bosch. We source OEM-compatible seals and complete replacement on-site.
