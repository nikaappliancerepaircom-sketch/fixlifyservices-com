Common Problems

Washer Problems We Fix in Queen Alexandra

Not Agitating
Broken agitator dogs or worn drive belt on top-loaders common in older Queen Alexandra homes. We carry replacement parts on the van.
Water Inlet Issues
Old copper supply lines and corroded inlet valves reduce fill speed or stop filling entirely. We test water pressure and replace valves.
Door Seal Leak
Front-loaders in damp basement infill suites develop boot seal mould and tears. Full seal replacement with mould remediation advice.
Lid Switch Failure
Top-loaders that won't start or drain often have a failed lid switch. Fast replacement, typically completed in the same visit.
Drain Pump Clog
Renovation debris and old lint clog pump filters in front-loaders. We clean the filter, inspect the pump impeller, and test drainage.
Noisy Spin Cycle
Worn drum bearings or loose tub fittings in aging machines. We diagnose bearing condition and advise repair vs. replace honestly.

Washer Care Tips for Queen Alexandra Homeowners

A well-maintained washer lasts 12–15 years. A neglected one typically fails at 7–9 years. Edmonton's hard water and cold winters add extra stress on washing machines in Queen Alexandra homes — here is how to extend your washer's life based on the repairs we see most often:

  • Monthly: Run a hot maintenance cycle with a washer cleaner tablet (Affresh works well in Edmonton's hard water). This removes detergent residue and mineral deposits that accumulate in the drum and pump filter.
  • Monthly: Leave the front-loader door ajar between cycles to prevent mould growth. Wipe the door boot gasket with a damp cloth — this is where most front-loader odours and seal deterioration start.
  • Quarterly: Check hose connections at the back of the unit. Edmonton's hard water can cause slow leaks at the connection point that go undetected for months before causing floor damage.
  • Always: Use HE detergent in HE washers. Standard detergent over-suds in low-water machines and leaves residue that accelerates drum bearing wear — one of the most common causes of the grinding noise we repair in Queen Alexandra homes.
  • Winter tip: If your washer is in an unheated space in Queen Alexandra, do not leave damp laundry in the drum when temperatures drop below −5°C — frozen residual water in the pump can cause pump failure on the next cycle.
