Oven Maintenance Tips to Prevent Costly Repairs

Regular maintenance extends your oven's lifespan and prevents the most common breakdowns we see in Etobicoke homes. Follow these five tips from our experienced technicians:

  1. Clean spills promptly ‚Äî baked-on grease causes smoke, odors, and can damage the oven interior coating over time
  2. Test temperature accuracy every 6 months with an oven thermometer ‚Äî a 25-degree drift is normal, but 50+ degrees means the sensor needs attention
  3. Inspect door gaskets annually ‚Äî a worn rubber seal lets heat escape, increases energy costs, and causes uneven baking
  4. Avoid using the self-clean cycle more than twice a year ‚Äî the extreme heat (900 F) stresses the door latch, control board, and thermal fuse
