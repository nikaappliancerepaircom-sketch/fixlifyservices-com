Why Appliance Repair Needs Purpose-Built Software

Appliance repair has specific operational requirements that generic CRM tools or project management apps don't address well. You're dealing with same-day or next-day service windows. Parts availability affects job scheduling. Brand-specific warranty terms affect what you can charge. Technician routes need to be efficient across a geographic service area. Diagnostic fees need to be collected at the door, not after the fact.

Software built for sales teams or software adapted from HVAC or plumbing solves some of these problems but not all of them. Fixlify was built for field service businesses — appliance repair among them — and that specificity shows in how the workflows are structured.

The Fixlify Platform: What It Does

AI Dispatcher — Capture Every Lead Automatically

The AI Dispatcher is the most transformative feature for most appliance repair businesses. Here's the core problem it solves: customers call when technicians are in the middle of jobs, during hours when staff aren't available, or they submit online inquiries that get buried in email. Every missed contact is a lost job.

The AI Dispatcher captures every call and online inquiry. It extracts the relevant information — appliance type, brand, problem description, customer location, preferred time window — and creates a structured job record. For multi-tech operations, it routes the inquiry to the right technician based on availability, location, and job type. You stop competing on who answers the phone first and start winning on who actually does the best repair work.

Online Booking for Modern Customers

A growing segment of appliance repair customers prefer self-service booking over calling. An embeddable Fixlify widget lets customers book online, pick a time slot, describe their problem, and enter their appliance details — all without calling. The job appears in your system fully structured and ready for dispatch. For technicians, this means arriving at jobs with complete information: model number, problem description, address, and confirmed appointment time.

End-to-End Job Management

Job management in Fixlify covers the complete lifecycle:

  1. Inquiry captured Call, online request, or walk-in — all routes create a record.
  2. Job created and assigned Technician assigned based on skills, location, and availability.
  3. Customer notified Automatic SMS confirmation and reminder sent.
  4. Technician dispatched On-my-way notification sent to customer automatically.
  5. On-site updates Tech updates job status from mobile app in real time.
  6. Parts ordered (if needed) Parts request tied to job. Return visit scheduled automatically.
  7. Job completed Tech marks complete. Invoice auto-generates. Payment collected on-site.
  8. Review requested Automated follow-up SMS/email sent after completion.

Customer CRM That Builds Long-Term Value

Every customer in Fixlify has a persistent profile. Every appliance they've had serviced is recorded. When a customer calls back — whether it's a warranty question sixty days later or a second appliance two years down the road — you have their complete history in front of you before the conversation starts.

For repeat business and referrals, this kind of institutional memory matters. Customers who feel recognized and valued refer more people. They're also easier to upsell on preventive maintenance or extended service plans when you can show them a clear history of what you've done for them.

Invoicing and Payment That Close the Loop

Appliance repair businesses lose money in two common ways: diagnostic fees that aren't collected because the collection process is awkward, and invoices that go out late because someone has to manually compile the job details after the fact. Fixlify's invoicing module auto-generates invoices when jobs are marked complete, with the correct line items already populated. Technicians can collect payment on-site via the mobile app — cash or card. Diagnostic fees become frictionless to collect.

Automated SMS and Email Communication

Appointment confirmations, reminders, on-my-way notifications, and post-job review requests all run automatically. You configure them once and they run without intervention. The review request automation alone consistently multiplies the monthly review volume for shops that implement it — and in local search, review volume and recency are among the most important ranking signals. More reviews from more satisfied customers in your service area means better visibility when someone searches for appliance repair near them.

Lead Pipeline Management

Not every inquiry converts to a booked job immediately. Some customers are getting multiple quotes. Some are waiting to see if their landlord will cover the repair. Some need time to decide between repair and replacement. Fixlify's lead pipeline tracks those conversations so they don't disappear. You follow up at the right time. More warm leads convert. Less revenue is lost to leads that went cold because no one remembered to call back.
