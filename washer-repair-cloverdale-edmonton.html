Common Washer Problems We Fix in Cloverdale

Not spinning / drum not turning
Drive belt, motor coupling, or motor control board failure on Kenmore top-loaders. Or bearing/spider arm failure on LG and Samsung front-loaders. Diagnosed and repaired same-day in most cases.
Not draining / water in drum
Drain pump clog or failure on Kenmore, Samsung, and LG. Front-load drain filters in Cloverdale homes frequently collect debris. We clear blockages and replace pumps when needed.
Leaking water
Door boot seal deterioration on LG and Samsung front-loaders is a common Cloverdale call. We source OEM-compatible seals and complete replacement on-site.
Loud banging or grinding
Drum bearing failure on front-loaders (5–10 years) or worn shock absorbers on older top-loaders. We diagnose and provide repair-value assessment before proceeding.
Not starting / lid or door fault
Lid switch on top-loaders, door latch on front-loaders, or control board fault. Diagnostic identifies the fault; most resolved same-day.
Error codes / UE / OE / dE
LG and Samsung front-loaders display fault codes. We decode and diagnose accurately. UE is usually load imbalance or bearing; OE is drain fault; dE is door latch.

Washer Care Tips for Cloverdale Edmonton Homeowners

A well-maintained washer lasts 12–15 years. A neglected one typically fails at 7–9 years. Edmonton's hard water and cold winters add extra stress on washing machines in Cloverdale Edmonton homes — here is how to extend your washer's life based on the repairs we see most often:

  • Monthly: Run a hot maintenance cycle with a washer cleaner tablet (Affresh works well in Edmonton's hard water). This removes detergent residue and mineral deposits that accumulate in the drum and pump filter.
  • Monthly: Leave the front-loader door ajar between cycles to prevent mould growth. Wipe the door boot gasket with a damp cloth — this is where most front-loader odours and seal deterioration start.
  • Quarterly: Check hose connections at the back of the unit. Edmonton's hard water can cause slow leaks at the connection point that go undetected for months before causing floor damage.
  • Always: Use HE detergent in HE washers. Standard detergent over-suds in low-water machines and leaves residue that accelerates drum bearing wear — one of the most common causes of the grinding noise we repair in Cloverdale Edmonton homes.
  • Winter tip: If your washer is in an unheated space in Cloverdale Edmonton, do not leave damp laundry in the drum when temperatures drop below −5°C — frozen residual water in the pump can cause pump failure on the next cycle.
