About Secord

Washer Repair Built for Secord Homes

Secord is a contemporary west Edmonton neighbourhood developed from the mid-2010s, offering modern homes across a range of formats — detached, semi-detached, and townhouse — at prices accessible to families at various stages of homeownership. The community's laundry appliances reflect the new-build era: Samsung front-loaders and LG direct-drive machines were installed as builder-package or early-ownership upgrades, with some Frigidaire and Whirlpool top-loaders rounding out the mix. With Secord homes now 5 to 10 years old, these washers are entering their highest-frequency repair window. Fixlify technicians reach Secord in approximately 20 minutes via Yellowhead Trail and 170 Street NW.

Samsung front-loaders in Secord generate 4C and 4E water inlet errors — codes that indicate insufficient water entering the machine — at an increasing rate after 6 to 8 years of service. Edmonton's moderately hard water at 130–160 mg/L causes progressive scaling of the inlet valve mesh screen; as the screen restricts water flow, the machine detects an insufficient fill rate and halts the cycle. Depending on the severity, we either clean the inlet screen (a 20-minute fix) or replace the valve assembly entirely. LG front-loaders present with UE (unbalanced load) errors from worn shock absorbers and drum bearings — a repair we carry parts for in every van serving west Edmonton neighbourhoods.

LG direct-drive washers in Secord may still be eligible for LG's 10-year motor warranty if purchased between 2014 and the present. Before any paid repair proceeds, our technician confirms the model number and approximate purchase date, verifies the fault is motor-related, and advises the homeowner on the LG warranty claim process. If the machine is within warranty, the motor or rotor cost is covered by LG — often saving $200 to $350. For machines outside warranty, including all Samsung, Frigidaire, and Whirlpool models, the $65 flat diagnostic applies and is credited in full against any repair performed during the same visit.
