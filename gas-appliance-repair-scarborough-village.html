Why must gas appliance repair be done by a TSSA-licensed technician?+

Ontario law prohibits unlicensed individuals from working on any gas-burning equipment. The TSSA licence confirms the technician completed a gas fitter apprenticeship, passed provincial combustion theory exams, and maintains active certification. Unlicensed gas work is illegal, voids home insurance, and creates genuine safety hazards including gas leaks and carbon monoxide exposure.
