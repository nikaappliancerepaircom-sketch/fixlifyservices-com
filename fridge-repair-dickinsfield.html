About Dickinsfield

Fridge Repair for Dickinsfield, Edmonton

Dickinsfield is an established northeast Edmonton neighbourhood from the 1960s and 1970s, and its homes have some of the oldest refrigerators still in service in the city. Frigidaire, GE, and Kenmore top-mount models from the 1970s and 1980s are not uncommon in Dickinsfield kitchens — these machines were built to last, and with relatively simple mechanical refrigeration systems, many continue to operate reliably. The most common faults in these vintage machines are start relay failures (a cheap, quick fix), thermostat failure causing temperature control loss, door gasket deterioration allowing warm air infiltration, and evaporator fan motor bearing wear. A failed start relay — often called the 'clicking fridge' problem — prevents the compressor from starting and is one of the most satisfying fixes in appliance repair: a $90–$130 part swap restores full cooling immediately. Our technicians have experience with the older Frigidaire direct-cooler and GE two-door top-mount platforms and can source parts through specialty aftermarket channels. Newer families in Dickinsfield who have upgraded have installed Samsung and LG French door fridges, which are equally within our service scope. Common Problems

Fridge Problems We Fix in Dickinsfield

Start Relay Clicking
Vintage Frigidaire and GE fridges click repeatedly and don't cool — the start relay has failed. Inexpensive fix, often completed in under 30 minutes.
Thermostat / Temperature Control
Mechanical thermostats in 1970s-80s fridges stick open or closed, causing freezing or warm temperature. We replace with new aftermarket thermostats.
Door Gasket Worn
40-50-year-old door gaskets become brittle and crack. We source replacement gaskets for vintage fridge profiles and install them same-day.
Evaporator Fan Motor
Bearing wear in vintage evaporator fan motors causes humming or stopping. We carry common replacement fan motor assemblies for GE and Kenmore platforms.
Defrost System (Older Fridges)
Older frost-free models develop defrost heater and thermostat faults causing frost accumulation. We replace the full defrost kit for lasting results.
Modern Samsung / LG Faults
Upgraded French door fridges in Dickinsfield homes develop ice maker, control board, and sealed system faults. We repair all current-generation brands.
