Maintenance Tips for Dishwasher Owners in Scarborough

Preventive maintenance is the most cost-effective way to extend your dishwasher's lifespan and avoid emergency repair calls. These five tips are specifically relevant for Scarborough homes, where older homes with outdated electrical panels that can stress modern appliances plays a significant role in appliance wear:

  1. Run a monthly cleaning cycle with a cup of white vinegar or a citric acid dishwasher cleaner to dissolve hard water scale
  2. Clean the drain filter every two weeks ‚Äî food debris trapped here is the number-one cause of drainage problems
  3. Inspect and wipe the door gasket monthly to prevent mould buildup and maintain a proper seal
  4. Scrape (do not pre-rinse) dishes before loading ‚Äî modern detergent needs some food residue to activate properly
  5. Leave the door cracked open after each cycle to let moisture escape and prevent interior odour and mildew

Following these steps can extend your dishwasher's life by 3-5 years and reduce the likelihood of an unexpected breakdown. If you notice anything unusual despite regular maintenance, book a diagnostic visit through Fixlify ‚Äî catching a small problem early almost always costs less than waiting until it fails completely.
