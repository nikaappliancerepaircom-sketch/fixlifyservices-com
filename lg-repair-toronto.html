LG Toronto FAQ

Do you repair LG refrigerators with Linear Compressor warranty issues?
Yes. LG's 10-year Linear Compressor warranty covers the compressor part but not labour. We handle LG compressor replacements and can advise on warranty claims. Call (437) 524-1053 to book.
My LG washer shows OE error. What does it mean?
OE means your LG washer is not draining. Check the pump filter (bottom-left panel) first — it collects lint and debris. If clearing the filter does not resolve it, the drain pump is likely failing. We fix LG OE errors same-day.
Can you fix LG ThinQ smart appliances?
Yes. LG ThinQ connectivity issues are separate from mechanical faults. We diagnose and repair the underlying hardware failure regardless of smart features. If needed, we guide you through ThinQ reconnection after the repair.
How much does LG appliance repair cost?
LG repairs typically cost $120 to $400. Linear compressor replacement is the most expensive LG repair at $350 to $500. Most other faults including drain pumps, ice makers, and heating elements run $150 to $350. Diagnostic fee ($119) waived with repair.
