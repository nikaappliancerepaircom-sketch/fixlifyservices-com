Common Samsung Appliance Problems We Fix

Our technicians have repaired thousands of Samsung appliances across Toronto and the GTA. These are the most frequent faults we encounter:

Ice Maker Failure

The most reported Samsung issue: ice maker stops producing ice due to a frozen fill tube, faulty water inlet valve, or defective ice maker assembly. Samsung RF and RB series models are particularly prone to this fault. We carry replacement ice maker assemblies for same-day repair.

Control Board Error Codes

Samsung refrigerators and washers display error codes (SE, 4E, 5E, dE, 1E) indicating specific component failures. Our technicians decode all Samsung error codes and repair the root cause rather than simply resetting the board.

Linear Compressor Noise (Fridges)

Samsung French Door models can develop compressor buzzing or clicking. This often indicates the compressor is overworking due to a dirty condenser coil or beginning to fail. Early diagnosis prevents a costly full compressor replacement.

Washer Door Lock Failure

Samsung front-load washers frequently develop door latch issues where the machine will not start or the door will not open after a cycle. We replace door lock assemblies on-site with OEM-compatible parts.

Dishwasher Not Draining

Samsung dishwashers display the 5C or OC error when the drain pump is clogged or failing. We clear pump blockages and replace drain pump assemblies as needed.

Oven Not Heating (Ranges)

Samsung electric ranges with failed bake or broil elements are a common call. We carry elements for popular Samsung NE and NX series ranges for fast same-day replacement.

My Samsung fridge ice maker stopped working. What is the fix?
Ice maker failure is the most common Samsung repair we handle. Frozen fill tubes, failed inlet valves, and defective ice maker assemblies are the usual causes. We diagnose and fix Samsung ice makers same-day with OEM-compatible parts.
