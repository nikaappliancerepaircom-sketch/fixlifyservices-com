About the Hamptons

Fridge Repair Built for Hamptons Homes

The Hamptons is northwest Edmonton's premier lake community, featuring custom-built homes on generous lots with premium interior appointments. Kitchens here reflect the neighbourhood's upscale character: Samsung 4-door Flex and French Door models, LG InstaView counter-depth units, KitchenAid Pro-series, and Miele integrated refrigerators are the appliances our technicians encounter most frequently. Fixlify reaches the Hamptons from our 10025 102A Avenue NW office in approximately 22 minutes via Anthony Henday Drive NW, enabling same-day service most days of the week.

Samsung French Door and 4-door Flex refrigerators in the Hamptons follow the pattern seen across Edmonton — the RF-series ice maker system is the most common failure point after four to six years of service. Frost bridges form around the auger motor, the thermistor loses calibration, and ice production stops or ice dispenses in broken clumps. Edmonton's water hardness at 130–160 mg/L accelerates scale deposition on the water inlet valve, compounding the issue. We carry Samsung ice maker modules, thermistors, and inlet valves on every van and resolve most ice maker calls in a single 90-minute appointment.

LG refrigerators in the Hamptons frequently benefit from LG's 10-year linear compressor warranty. If your LG fridge is under 10 years old and the compressor has failed, LG covers the part — often at zero cost to you. We assess the machine on-site, confirm whether the compressor is the fault, and guide you through the LG warranty claim process before any out-of-pocket repair begins. For Miele integrated units, KitchenAid Pro-series, and older Whirlpool or Maytag models outside of any manufacturer warranty, our $65 diagnostic applies and is credited against the repair total.
