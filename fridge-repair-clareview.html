What We Fix

Common Fridge Faults in Clareview

Fridge Not Cooling

The most urgent fridge fault — contents at risk. Causes range from a failed evaporator fan, a frosted-over evaporator coil blocking airflow, a failing compressor, or a low refrigerant charge. We diagnose methodically, starting with the most likely and most repairable causes.

Samsung Ice Maker Frost-Over

Samsung French-door ice makers are prone to frost accumulation around the ice maker assembly, causing the ice maker to stop producing. A defrost heater upgrade kit resolves the root cause — not just the symptom — and is the correct long-term fix for this well-documented issue.

LG Compressor Failure

Certain LG French-door and side-by-side models have linear compressors with documented early failure rates. Before recommending compressor replacement (a $400–$600 job), we confirm the diagnosis and check whether the unit is still within LG's extended compressor warranty.

Evaporator Fan Motor Failure

A loud buzzing or grinding noise from inside the fridge — especially noticeable when opening the door — is typically a failing evaporator fan. The freezer stays cold but the fridge compartment warms up. Usually a $130–$200 repair.

Water Dispenser / Ice Maker Line Freeze

Edmonton's cold winters cause water supply lines to freeze if the fridge is near an exterior wall. We thaw the line, check the water inlet valve, and advise on insulation to prevent recurrence — a common issue in Clareview apartments near poorly insulated exterior walls.

Door Gasket Seal Failure

Worn or torn door gaskets allow warm air to infiltrate the fridge compartment, forcing the compressor to run continuously and raising energy bills. Gasket replacement is $80–$140 and typically solves the problem within minutes of fitting.

Common Fridge Problems We Fix in Clareview

Refrigerator repair calls in Clareview peak in two seasons: summer, when compressors work harder against the heat, and late fall, when Edmonton's temperature drops rapidly and garage fridges begin cycling abnormally. These are the faults our technicians encounter most often in Clareview:

  • →Fridge not cooling or not cold enough — compressor, thermostat, evaporator fan, or condenser coil failure. The evaporator fan is the most common single-point failure on modern frost-free units in Clareview.
  • →Ice building up on the back wall — a failed defrost heater or defrost thermostat causes ice to accumulate on the evaporator. This blocks airflow and the fridge gradually warms up. Very common in Clareview during winter months.
  • →Water dispenser or ice maker not working — a frozen fill line or failed water inlet valve. Edmonton homes with in-door dispensers see frozen lines regularly during the coldest weeks of winter.
  • →Fridge running constantly or spiking energy bill — a worn door gasket lets cold air escape and forces the compressor to run continuously. Test: close the door on a sheet of paper — if it slides out easily, the gasket is failing.
  • →Clicking or not starting — a failing start relay is an inexpensive fix ($60–$110) that prevents a full compressor replacement ($450–$750). Catching it early saves significant money.

A non-functioning fridge is a food safety emergency. We prioritize same-day service for fridge calls in Clareview — book before noon and we aim to arrive that afternoon.
