Common issues

Common Fridge Problems We Fix in Jasper Place

Not cooling / warm fridge
Condenser coil blockage, evaporator fan failure, or compressor fault. Common in older Kenmore and GE top-freezer units in Jasper Place. We diagnose the cooling system fully and advise on repair vs replacement for units over 15 years old.
Ice maker not making ice
Ice maker module failure, frozen water supply line, or inlet valve fault. Very common in Samsung and LG French-door units installed in renovated Jasper Place homes. Usually a same-day fix with parts on the truck.
Frost build-up / defrost failure
Defrost heater, defrost thermostat, or defrost timer failure causes ice accumulation on the evaporator coil. Fridge section loses cooling while freezer stays cold. Common in top-freezer GE and Kenmore units in Jasper Place.
Water dispenser not working
Water inlet valve failure or frozen dispenser line — common in French-door units in Edmonton's winter climate. We thaw, test, and replace the inlet valve or dispenser module as needed on Samsung, LG, and Whirlpool platforms.
Fridge too warm, freezer fine
Evaporator fan motor failure or damper door fault blocks cold air flow from the freezer to the fresh food section. Diagnostic distinguishes between fan failure and airflow blockage — both are same-day repairs in most cases.
Compressor noise / clicking
Start relay failure causes clicking and intermittent compressor operation — inexpensive fix. True compressor failure is costly and we advise on economics. Inverter compressor issues on newer LG and Samsung units require specific diagnostic tools.
