LG Refrigerator Repair — Common Issues We Fix

Our technicians have extensive experience with LG refrigerator repair faults. We diagnose accurately and repair on the first visit in most cases.

Linear Compressor Failure

LG offers a 10-year warranty on its Linear Compressor, but compressors can still fail outside warranty. Symptoms include the fridge not cooling, warm temperatures despite the compressor running, or a clicking noise. We service LG sealed systems and carry refrigerant.

Fridge Ice Maker Not Making Ice

LG French Door and InstaView refrigerators develop ice maker faults from frozen auger assemblies, failed inlet valves, or defective ice maker modules. LG's Craft Ice maker (round ice) has its own unique fault pattern we diagnose specifically.

LG Refrigerator Repair in Toronto — Linear Compressor and Common Faults

LG refrigerators — particularly the LRMVS, LRFVS, and LFXS series French-door models — are the most common premium refrigerator repair call in Toronto's condos and renovated homes. LG's linear compressor technology offers energy efficiency advantages, but a significant number of linear compressors manufactured between 2014 and 2018 developed premature failures. LG extended the compressor warranty to 10 years following class-action proceedings, and many Toronto homeowners with affected units are still entitled to warranty compressor replacement. Fixlify assists Toronto homeowners in identifying whether their LG model falls within the affected serial number range before recommending any repair approach.

For LG refrigerators outside the compressor warranty period, the most common Toronto service calls involve defrost system failures. LG's dual-evaporator French-door models use a sophisticated defrost schedule managed by the Micom control board. When the defrost heater burns out (Er dH code), ice accumulates on the fresh-food evaporator until the Er FF fan code follows — the fan can no longer rotate through the ice. Toronto homeowners see both the fresh-food section warm and hear unusual noise from the fridge before the fans stop completely. The repair requires a manual defrost cycle (2–3 hours with the doors open or using a heat gun), replacement of the failed heater, and re-verification of the thermistor circuit. Fixlify technicians complete this repair same-day in most cases.

LG InstaView Door-in-Door refrigerators, common in Toronto's upscale new-build condos and renovated Annex kitchens, require specialised handling. The door-in-door panel has a separate hinge, seal, and electrical connector for the InstaView LED panel. When the InstaView display stops responding to knocking, the fault is usually a failed door switch or LED driver module in the door panel — not the main control board. We carry LG InstaView door panel modules for same-day replacement. All LG refrigerator repairs by Fixlify carry a 90-day parts and labour warranty regardless of the repair type.
