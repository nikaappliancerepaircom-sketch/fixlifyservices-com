How Our Appliance Repair Service Works

Fixlify Appliance Services was built around online booking and transparent repair. The moment you book at fixlifyservices.com/book or call (437) 524-1053, our dispatch system matches you with the closest available certified technician who carries parts for your appliance brand. You'll receive an instant text and email confirmation with your technician's name and an estimated arrival time.

When our technician arrives, the first step is a thorough diagnostic inspection of the problem — not guessing, but a systematic evaluation of the most likely failure points for your specific appliance model. Before any repair work begins, you'll receive a written upfront quote that covers all parts and labour. There are no hidden fees and no surprises. If you decide not to proceed, you pay only the diagnostic fee. If you approve the repair, we get to work immediately — most appliances are fixed within 60–90 minutes on the first visit.

Every repair we perform comes with a 90-day parts and labour warranty. If the same issue returns within 90 days, we come back and fix it at no charge. We use genuine OEM parts or certified equivalent components from trusted suppliers — not low-cost aftermarket substitutes that fail prematurely. Our technicians are licensed under Ontario's technical standards regulations, gas-certified where required, fully insured, and background-checked.

Same-day service is available across Toronto, Scarborough, North York, Etobicoke, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Richmond Hill, Oakville, Burlington, Pickering, Ajax, Whitby, and Oshawa. Book any time at fixlifyservices.com/book — our online calendar shows real-time availability 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
