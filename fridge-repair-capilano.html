Common Fridge Faults in Capilano

Defrost System Failure (GE/Kenmore)

The most common repair on older Capilano fridges. Defrost heater, thermostat, or timer failure leads to frost buildup on the evaporator coil, blocking airflow and causing the fridge section to warm up. Defrost system components are inexpensive and we typically have them on the van.

LG Ice Maker Frost-Over

LG French-door ice makers in Capilano homes develop frost accumulation that stops ice production. Edmonton's temperature swings between seasons create humidity cycling that accelerates the problem. Defrost heater upgrade kit is the correct solution — not a manual defrost that recurs in weeks.

Evaporator Fan Motor

A buzzing noise from the back of the fridge or freezer section that stays cold while the fridge warms up typically indicates a failing evaporator fan. We replace the motor with a compatible part — usually same-day completion.

Samsung French-Door Ice Maker

Samsung French-door ice makers have the same frost-over tendency as LG. We install the defrost heater upgrade kit that Samsung released as a documented fix for this issue — addressing the root cause rather than just clearing the frost temporarily.

Door Gasket Replacement

Older fridges in Capilano with hardened or torn door gaskets run their compressors constantly, raising electricity bills and shortening compressor life. A replacement gasket ($80–$130) is one of the most cost-effective fridge repairs available.

Water Line Freezing (Winter)

Fridges in Capilano kitchens near exterior walls can have water supply lines that freeze during Edmonton winters, stopping ice maker and water dispenser function. We thaw the line and assess whether insulation or line rerouting is needed to prevent recurrence.

FAQ

Fridge Repair Questions — Capilano

My LG fridge ice maker stopped working — what's wrong? +
LG French-door ice makers commonly stop producing ice due to frost accumulation around the ice maker assembly. Edmonton's climate cycling — cold winters and warm summer humidity — makes this worse. The correct fix is a defrost heater upgrade kit (not just a manual defrost), which LG released specifically for this documented issue. Cost is typically $155–$255 including installation.
How do I know if my fridge needs a defrost system repair? +
Signs include: heavy frost buildup inside the freezer section, the fridge section warming up while the freezer stays cold, the freezer fan making a louder than normal noise (frost blocking the fan blade), or the fridge running constantly. Any of these symptoms typically point to a defrost heater, thermostat, or timer failure.

Is it worth repairing a 15-year-old Kenmore fridge? +

We give you the specific fault cost and let you decide — for defrost system components or an evaporator fan, repair almost always makes sense financially.

Common Fridge Problems We Fix in Capilano

Refrigerator repair calls in Capilano peak in two seasons: summer, when compressors work harder against the heat, and late fall, when Edmonton's temperature drops rapidly and garage fridges begin cycling abnormally. These are the faults our technicians encounter most often in Capilano:

  • →Fridge not cooling or not cold enough — compressor, thermostat, evaporator fan, or condenser coil failure. The evaporator fan is the most common single-point failure on modern frost-free units in Capilano.
  • →Ice building up on the back wall — a failed defrost heater or defrost thermostat causes ice to accumulate on the evaporator. This blocks airflow and the fridge gradually warms up. Very common in Capilano during winter months.
  • →Water dispenser or ice maker not working — a frozen fill line or failed water inlet valve. Edmonton homes with in-door dispensers see frozen lines regularly during the coldest weeks of winter.
  • →Fridge running constantly or spiking energy bill — a worn door gasket lets cold air escape and forces the compressor to run continuously. Test: close the door on a sheet of paper — if it slides out easily, the gasket is failing.
  • →Clicking or not starting — a failing start relay is an inexpensive fix ($60–$110) that prevents a full compressor replacement ($450–$750). Catching it early saves significant money.

A non-functioning fridge is a food safety emergency. We prioritize same-day service for fridge calls in Capilano — book before noon and we aim to arrive that afternoon.
