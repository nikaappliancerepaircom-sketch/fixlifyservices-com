Samsung dishwasher no-wash / drain error

Samsung DW-series in builder packages across Coloniale and Eaglemont. Drain pump debris and circulation motor wear at 5–10 years. Pump replacement + limescale clean; we carry Samsung pumps on every Beaumont run.

Whirlpool washer F5 lid lock fault

GE Profile fridge control board error
