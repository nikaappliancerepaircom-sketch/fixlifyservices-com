Gas Oven Safety Precautions

A gas oven contains a sealed combustion chamber within your Toronto kitchen. Toronto Fire Services responds to approximately 300 gas-related calls annually across the city. Failed ignition can allow raw gas to accumulate — here is how to respond safely.

  1. If your Toronto gas oven smells like gas but the igniter did not light, turn the oven off and keep the door closed
  2. Ventilate your kitchen — open windows and activate the range hood exhaust
  3. Wait at least 10 minutes before any attempt to use the oven again
  4. If the gas smell continues with all controls off, evacuate and dial 911 from outside
  5. Contact Enbridge Gas at 1-866-763-5427 about the suspected leak
