Glenridding Washer Repair — Common Questions

My LG washer shows UE — is it covered under warranty?+
Possibly. LG offers a 10-year motor warranty on models purchased from 2010 onward. UE errors can stem from worn shock absorbers (not covered) or an inverter motor fault (potentially covered). Our technician verifies your warranty status before quoting any paid repair.
What causes Samsung DC error in a Glenridding home?+
DC error indicates the drum cannot balance during spin. In Glenridding's Samsung units, the primary cause is worn drum bearings or shock absorbers that allow excessive drum movement. Bearing replacement runs $180–$280 and is completed in a single visit.
